# Editing your Elevate website

Everything on the site can be changed from one place — no code, no developer needed.

## Logging in
1. Go to **your-website.com/admin**
2. Click **Login with GitHub** (use the account you were invited with).
3. You're in. Changes you make and **Publish** go live on the real site in about a minute.

## What you can edit

### 🎧 Roster (DJs)
Open **Roster (DJs)**. To **add** an artist, click **New DJ**, fill in the fields, and
**upload a photo** (portrait photos look best). To **edit** or **remove** one, click their
name. Use the **Sort order** number to control who appears first (lower = earlier).
- *Available for booking?* toggles the green "available" / "booked out" badge.
- *Genres* drive both the tags on their card and the filter buttons on the roster page.

### 📇 Contact, socials & info
Open **Site settings → Contact, socials & info** to change your **email, Instagram,
SoundCloud, location**, the **genre list**, the home-page **ticker** and **stats**, and
the footer/navigation links. These update everywhere on the site at once.

### ✍️ Page text
Open **Page content** and pick a page (Home, Roster, How it works, Contact) to edit its
**headlines, paragraphs, buttons** and **SEO** (the title/description shown in Google and
link previews).

## Saving
Click **Save**, then **Publish** (or "Publish now"). Wait ~1 minute, then refresh the live
site. That's it.

## Tips
- Headlines that span two lines: just press Enter for the line break.
- Keep photos a sensible size (a phone photo is fine; avoid huge files).
- Made a mistake? Every change is saved in history and can be rolled back — ask your
  developer if you ever need that.
